# FALSIFICATION

**Transition:** EXPLORATION → PRESENT + AKAL

Falsification is the engine of epistemic progress. A hypothesis meets evidence. AKAL evaluates whether the evidence supports or contradicts. The result is not "truth" — it is "survived for now" or "eliminated."

Examples: BEKANTAN-1 well test calibrating basin model.

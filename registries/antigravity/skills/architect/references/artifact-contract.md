# Artifact Contract

All artifacts generated by the Architect must follow this standard format to ensure seamless handoff to Forge and RSI roles across the Warga AAA ecosystem.

## Required Sections
1. **Context & Motivation**: Why are we doing this?
2. **Acceptance Criteria**: Verifiable conditions for success.
3. **Task Graph / Execution Plan**: Step-by-step breakdown.
4. **Constraints & Assumptions**: What must NOT change, and what we assume to be true.
5. **Open Risks**: Remaining unknowns.

So What?: What this means for you

As fuel subsidies drop in May and data center power needs rise, both the cost of living and doing business will shift significantly. Expect PETRONAS to squeeze every drop of efficiency out of existing fields using AI, while the broader economy adjusts to the true cost of energy.

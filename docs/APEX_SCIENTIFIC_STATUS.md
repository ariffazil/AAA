1. **Measurable variables** with units and ranges  
2. **Operational definitions** (not only names)  
3. **Calibration procedures** against ground truth / outcomes  
4. **Falsifiable predictions** stated in advance  
5. **Experiments** that could prove it wrong  
6. **Independent replication** of results  

Until then: **meta-framework / governance research programme**.

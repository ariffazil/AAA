## Falsification checklist (before SEAL)

1. What would prove this claim false?  
2. Was an alternative interpretation recorded?  
3. Is evidence time-stamped and source-tagged?  
4. Did any organ contradict?  
5. Was score averaged over a fatal veto? (forbidden)  
6. Can an external party re-run the computation?

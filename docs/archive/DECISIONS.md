# DECISIONS.md — Sealed Decision Log

> **Purpose:** Structured audit trail for consequential decisions.
> **DITEMPA BUKAN DIBERI — Intelligence is forged, not given.**

**MEMORY.md** = durable context and lessons
**DECISIONS.md** = sealed, traceable decisions

Every consequential action gets a formal entry here.
Routine work does not. When in doubt, log it.

---

## Decision Entry Format

```markdown
## YYYY-MM-DD — [Short title]

**Decision:** What was decided
**Reason:** Why this path was chosen
**Authority:** Who approved (Arif / 888 Judge / OPENCLAW L3+)
**Files affected:** What was changed
**Risk reduced:** What danger this prevents
**Status:** Sealed | Superseded | Rolled back
**Supersedes:** [link to earlier decision if applicable]
**Superseded by:** [link if later decision overrides]
**Notes:** [optional]
```

---

## Archive (superseded decisions)

Old decisions move here when superseded.
Do not delete — the archive is the audit trail.

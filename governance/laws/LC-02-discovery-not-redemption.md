# LC-02: Discovery Is Not Redemption
## VAULT999::LAW::SEALED::2026-06-19

**LAW:** A discovery does not redeem a failed objective.

**ORIGIN SCAR:** [#3A] BUNGA TASBIH-1 (PM 328, 2019–2020)

**EVIDENCE:**
- Primary Oligocene Syn-rift objective: water-bearing (P50 syn-rift = 0)
- Secondary Miocene Post-rift: 3.6m net oil J-70/J-80 + 1m I-125, non-commercial
- Post-MEFS cutoff 2.6 MMstb — well fell short
- Post-drill: "Bunga Tasbih-1 downgraded the remaining Syn-rift play potential in the sub-basin"
- The institution labeled it an "oil discovery"

**PARADOX:** A well drilled to fulfill a PSC commitment found non-commercial oil and downgraded the play it was supposed to prove. The label "discovery" masked the condemnation.

**COROLLARY:** Not every discovery expands a play. Some close it honestly. And some do both — find oil on one objective while killing the other.
